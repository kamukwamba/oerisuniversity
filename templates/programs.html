Programs

OCERIES UNIVERSITY COLLEGE OF METAPHYSICAL SCIENCES LUSAKA CAMPUS - ZAMBIA Oceries University College of Metaphysical Sciences has three departments, namely Department of Media and Journalism Studies, Department of Business studies and Department of Metaphysical Sciences. Courses are offered by each department based on its mandate. The University offers Twelve [12] programmes at Certificate level, six [6] programs at Diploma level and Three [3] programs at Bachelors level. Other programmes are three [3] at Master’s level and three [3] at PhD level. For the award of any Certificate at any level, an aspiring student is required to pass all the courses and meet the minimum requirements set by Oceries University Senate in the hours spent studying the course and fees related to the course. Graduation will be conducted bi-annually, in the month of July and the month of December. The graduation will be preceded by Oceries University Board of Examiners to review all courses examined. The mode of delivery for the courses is the British English. To enroll, fill in the forms online and pay the relevant fees.

CERTIFICATE COURSES

At Certificate level, the student is expected to spent at least 90 days studying the relevant course materials provided by Oceries University. The student is also expected to pay relevant fees of USD 5 for registration and tuition fee of USD 50. Fees can be paid using the local currency in the bank account provided by the Oceries University.
