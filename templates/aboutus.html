About Us

The University College of Metaphysical Sciences was established in 2024 and the first intake took place on 1st September 2024. UCMS offers online courses, through text, audio and video and currently has students coming from corners of the world. UCMS’s campus is based in Lusaka, Zambia where programmes are coordinated. The Lusaka Campus has a Library, where registered Metaphysical Students can access material for their course.

Scope
Oceries University of Metaphysical Sciences (OUMS) Strategic Plan sets out a framework of how the university will operate in the period 2025 to 2030. The contents of the strategic plan have been put in place after a wider consultation with various stakeholders involved in the smooth running of the university. The strategic plan is underpinned by a more detailed annual implementation plan which is approved by the University Council.


Mission
To provide relevant tertiary education to all those seeking to be enlightened


Vision
To awaken humanity to understand the purpose and mission on earth through pursuit of knowledge, Research and Innovation.

Core Values

Alternative thinking: Oceries University provides an opportunity to students and graduates to consider unconventional, innovative, or unorthodox ideas and perspectives in finding solutions to the intricates of life. This involves thinking outside the box, challenging assumptions and exploring new possibilities.

Academic Freedom: Oceries University embraces the rights and privileges of academics, researchers to pursue knowledge, express ideas and engage in intellectual inquiry without undue restrictions, censorship or interference. In all this the University will encourage integrity, quality and progress of academic research and education.


Academic Integrity: Oceries University will ensure ethical principles and values guide academic behavior, ensuring that academic work is conducted honestly, responsibly and with intellectual rigor. Oceries will uphold the highest standards of academic conduct, avoiding dishonesty, and respecting the intellectual property of others.


Academic Innovativeness: Oceries University will ensure that academics, researchers and students generate and implement new ideas, methods and approaches in their research, teaching and academic practices. This will entail thinking creatively, taking risks, and experimenting with new concepts, technologies, and techniques to advance knowledge, improve education and address complex problems.


Academic Excellence: Oceries University will strive for achievement of exceptionally high standards in academic pursuits, characterized by a deep understanding, critical

STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES

STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE ONE:


To admit students from all backgrounds with suitable qualification to pursue and benefit from the university education.

The University’s aim is to ensure that prospecting students regardless of their physical locations benefit from the courses that are offered by the University online. The recruitment process therefore embraces all prospecting students regardless of their physical locations as long as they meet the academic requirements set by the university. The delivery method is strictly online, with lecturers and supervisors readily available to offer support to students virtually. In very special and rare cases, prospecting students will be allowed to physically interact with the Lecturers and Supervisors

To achieve this, the university will strengthen its presence on various social media platforms. The University will work with other colleges and Universities, academic departments and faculties to ensure effective coordination of outreach activity. The University will enhance the undergraduate and graduate admissions processes to ensure equality of opportunity for all applicants, and to improve efficiency and quality of service.
