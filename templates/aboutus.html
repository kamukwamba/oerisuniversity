About Us

The University College of Metaphysical Sciences was established in 2024 and the first intake took place on 1st September 2024. UCMS offers online courses, through text, audio and video and currently has students coming from corners of the world. UCMS’s campus is based in Lusaka, Zambia where programmes are coordinated. The Lusaka Campus has a Library, where registered Metaphysical Students can access material for their course.

OCERIES STRATEGIC PLAN

A message from the Principal

Dear Friends and all stakeholders,


I am pleased to present to you Oceries University of Metaphysicla Sciences strategic plan, which was developed in consultation with students, stakeholders and the institution faculty. The Strategic Plan, presents the vision and mission of Oceries University of Metaphysical Sciences which is anchored on the objectives. The University has Four objectives, which is To admit students from all backgrounds with suitable qualification to pursue and benefit from the university education.

The second objective is to offer a conducive academic environment for all students and graduates to excel during and after leaving the university.

The third objective is to to Enhance Strength and Stability of the university in all the academic work.

Blessings to you all!

Dr. Felix Kunda Principal, OUMS

Introduction

Welcome to our strategic plan for the Oceries University of Metaphysical Sciences. The Strategic Plan will cover the period from 2025 to 2030. Oceries University of Metaphysical Sciences, brings on the global level education that provides an opportunity for all to learn regardless of their physical locations.


Oceries University of Metaphysical Sciences is being recognized nationally and globally as a leading provider in higher education, with numerous indicators of the positive impact on the students and the world.


The University is now poised to build on these strengths by taking the bold decisions to respond to the emerging trends at global level, while remaining true to awaken humanity is purpose of life.


The University was founded in 2024, in Zambia with a focus of providing education at global level. The university has realized that we now live in an information world with more technologically complex, a world in which communication, discovery, and change occur at a pace unimagined an opportunity Oceries has embraced.


Our plan for the University’s next chapter of excellence is based on extensive consultation with campus stakeholders. The plan highlights four Strategic objectives, that will organize

the University’s efforts to maintain our strength and grow where opportunities arise to support student success and contribute to our disciplines, to the nation, and to the world.


This strategic plan is written broadly, to provide guidance and not constraint. The Strategic Directions, and the Core Values they express, are central to the success of Oceries University of Metaphysical Sciences and its students. Our intention is to provide a framework that will lead to creativity and identify the ways that will help move the University toward our collective Vision.


A university-wide Implementation Team will coordinate accountability efforts and develop an assessment plan by which we will track our successes and identify where we need to do more or better as Oceries University of Metaphysical Sciences.


The Strategic plan provides a framework for the University to exhibit its characteristic financial discipline, while making the most of our resources in the service of our Mission and the advancement of our Vision.
