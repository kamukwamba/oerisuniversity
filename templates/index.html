About Us

Oceries University College of Metaphysical Sciences was established in 2024 and the first intake took place on 1st September 2024. OUCMS offers online courses, through text, audio and video and currently has students coming from all corners of the world. OUCMS campus is based in Lusaka, Zambia where programmes are coordinated. The Lusaka Campus has a Library, where registered Metaphysical Students can access material for their course.
